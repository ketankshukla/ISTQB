### Q1-Q10

1. Finance testing includes: A) Only UI B) Accuracy, compliance, security C) Only speed D) Only colors — **Accuracy, compliance, security**
2. Core banking processes: A) Only trading B) Deposits, withdrawals, transfers C) Only insurance D) Only FX — **Deposits, withdrawals, transfers**
3. A futures contract: A) Right to buy B) Obligation to buy/sell C) Bond D) Insurance — **Obligation to buy/sell**
4. Payment authorization checks: A) Only card color B) Funds, validity, fraud C) Only address D) Only name — **Funds, validity, fraud**
5. SOX requires: A) Customer surveys B) Internal controls C) Only training D) Only marketing — **Internal controls**
6. PCI-DSS applies to: A) All software B) Card data systems C) Only trading D) Only banks — **Card data systems**
7. Simple interest formula: A) P(1+r)^n B) P×r×t C) P/r D) P+r — **P×r×t**
8. A limit buy at $40 when market is $45: A) Executes B) Waits until $40 or below C) Rejected D) Executes at $45 — **Waits until $40 or below**
9. Chargeback: A) Early payment B) Customer dispute reversal C) Price increase D) Bank merger — **Customer dispute reversal**
10. VWAP strategy: A) Time-weighted B) Volume-weighted C) Price-only D) Random — **Volume-weighted**
