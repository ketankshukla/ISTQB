### 🔷 Q1-Q10

1. Which is a front-office function: A) Settlement B) Order execution C) Reconciliation D) Regulatory reporting — **Order execution**
2. A certificate of deposit is: A) Checking account B) Time deposit with fixed term C) Money market D) Credit card — **Time deposit with fixed term**
3. A call option gives: A) Obligation to buy B) Right to buy C) Right to sell D) Obligation to sell — **Right to buy**
4. Front office is concerned with: A) Monthly reconciliation B) Real-time execution and market data C) Year-end tax D) Payroll — **Real-time execution and market data**
5. SWIFT used for: A) Domestic ACH B) International payment messaging C) Crypto trading D) Social media — **International payment messaging**
6. General ledger: A) Social media B) All financial transactions C) Marketing D) Building access — **All financial transactions**
7. Key bond attributes: A) Dividend yield B) Coupon rate and maturity C) P/E ratio D) Market cap — **Coupon rate and maturity**
8. Back office functions: A) Real-time execution B) Settlement and reconciliation C) Market analysis D) Prospecting — **Settlement and reconciliation**
9. Card issuer is: A) Acquirer B) Issuer C) Processor D) Gateway — **Issuer**
10. Overdraft protection: A) CD B) Checking account C) Bond D) Insurance policy — **Checking account**
