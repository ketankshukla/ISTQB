### 🔷 Q1-Q10

1. The PRIMARY objective of finance testing is: A) UI design B) Ensure financial systems are accurate, compliant, secure, and performant C) Reduce costs D) Speed up development — **Ensure financial systems are accurate, compliant, secure, and performant**
2. A core banking system component that calculates interest is: A) Fee Engine B) Interest Engine C) Loan Management D) General Ledger — **Interest Engine**
3. A bond's coupon rate represents: A) Market price B) Interest payment rate C) Maturity date D) Credit rating — **Interest payment rate**
4. The middle office is responsible for: A) Executing trades B) Risk management and compliance monitoring C) Settlement D) Customer onboarding — **Risk management and compliance monitoring**
5. Real-time payments operate: A) Only business hours B) 24/7 with instant settlement C) Only weekdays D) With 3-day delay — **24/7 with instant settlement**
6. SOX Section 404 requires: A) Data encryption B) Internal controls over financial reporting C) Best execution D) AML monitoring — **Internal controls over financial reporting**
7. MiFID II requires trade timestamps accurate to: A) Seconds B) Milliseconds C) Microseconds D) Minutes — **Microseconds**
8. Enhanced Due Diligence is for: A) All customers B) High-risk customers C) Only corporate accounts D) Only retail — **High-risk customers**
9. Value-at-Risk measures: A) Credit risk B) Market risk C) Operational risk D) Liquidity risk — **Market risk**
10. Basel III requires: A) Only liquidity ratios B) Capital adequacy, liquidity, leverage limits C) Only training records D) Only satisfaction scores — **Capital adequacy, liquidity, leverage limits**

### 🔷 Q11-Q20

11. A Suspicious Activity Report is filed under: A) SOX B) AML C) PCI-DSS D) GDPR — **AML**
12. Dirty price of a bond: A) Only clean price B) Clean price plus accrued interest C) Only coupon D) Only face value — **Clean price plus accrued interest**
13. Delta measures: A) Time decay B) Sensitivity to underlying price C) Sensitivity to volatility D) Rate of change of delta — **Sensitivity to underlying price**
14. 1-day 95% VaR of $1M means: A) Max loss $1M B) 5% chance of losing more than $1M C) Expected loss $1M D) Losses always less than $1M — **5% chance of losing more than $1M**
15. Using float for currency is risky because: A) Too much memory B) Precision errors C) Too slow D) Not supported — **Precision errors**
16. In amortization, early payments are mostly: A) Principal B) Interest C) Fees D) Taxes — **Interest**
17. A zero-coupon bond: A) Pays semiannual coupons B) No periodic interest; return from discount C) No maturity D) Variable rate — **No periodic interest; return from discount**
18. VWAP stands for: A) Volume-Weighted Average Price B) Time-Weighted Average Price C) Value-Weighted Asset Portfolio D) Variable-Window Algorithmic Pricing — **Volume-Weighted Average Price**
19. In payment processing, actual fund transfer is: A) Authorization B) Clearing C) Settlement D) Reconciliation — **Settlement**
20. A reconciliation break occurs when: A) Systems agree B) Two systems show different values C) Trade canceled D) New employee joins — **Two systems show different values**

26. Ex-dividend date: A) Date paid B) First day trading without dividend C) Date declared D) Annual meeting — **First day trading without dividend**
27. Stop-loss at $45, price drops to $44: A) Canceled B) Becomes market order and executes C) Inactive D) Executes at $45 — **Becomes market order and executes**
