# Chapter 3 — Regulatory Compliance and Risk Management

## Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~20% (~8 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K1, K2, K3
- **Focus:** SOX, MiFID II, Basel III, AML/KYC, risk types, compliance testing

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Identify major financial regulations and their testing implications
2. Describe SOX internal controls and testing requirements
3. Explain MiFID II transaction reporting requirements
4. Describe AML and KYC processes
5. Identify types of financial risk (credit, market, operational, liquidity)
6. Design tests for regulatory compliance

---

## Key Concepts

- SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act)
- MiFID II
- Basel III
- AML (Anti-Money Laundering) and KYC (Know Your Customer)
- PCI-DSS
- GDPR in financial context
- Financial risk types
- Compliance testing approaches
