## ⭐ Tips for Maximum Efficiency

- **Active recall over passive reading.** After reading a lesson section, close it and write down the key points from memory before moving on.
- **Technique drilling is non-negotiable.** Chapters 4 and 5 are where points are won. Practice deriving test cases until the steps are automatic.
- **Time yourself on practice questions.** Build the habit of 90 seconds per question.
- **Review wrong answers deeply.** Understand why each distractor is wrong, not just why the correct answer is right.
- **Use the GLOSSARY.md** as flashcard material. ISTQB exams test precise terminology.
