# 📚 ISTQB CTFL v4.0 — Extended Practice Exam Set (50 Exams)

This folder contains **50 full-length practice exams** plus **50 matching answer keys**, created to give you deep, repeatable preparation for the ISTQB Certified Tester Foundation Level (CTFL) v4.0 examination.

## 🎯 How These Were Built

Every question is grounded in the **official CTFL v4.0 syllabus** (released 21 April 2023, mandatory from 9 May 2024) and its **64 Learning Objectives** across 6 chapters. Each exam mirrors the **official Exam Structure Tables v1.6** so that practicing on them feels exactly like the real thing.

### Official exam blueprint (followed by every exam)

| Chapter | Topic | Questions | K1 | K2 | K3 |
|---------|-------|-----------|----|----|----|
| 1 | Fundamentals of Testing | 8 | 2 | 6 | 0 |
| 2 | Testing Throughout the SDLC | 6 | 2 | 4 | 0 |
| 3 | Static Testing | 4 | 2 | 2 | 0 |
| 4 | Test Analysis and Design | 11 | 0 | 6 | 5 |
| 5 | Managing the Test Activities | 9 | 1 | 5 | 3 |
| 6 | Test Tools | 2 | 1 | 1 | 0 |
| **Total** | | **40** | **8** | **24** | **8** |

- **40 questions**, **60 minutes**, **pass mark 65% (26/40)**
- **K-levels**: K1 (remember), K2 (understand), K3 (apply)
- The 8 K3 (apply) questions appear in Chapters 4 and 5 — equivalence partitioning, boundary value analysis, decision tables, state transition testing, statement/branch coverage, test case prioritization, risk-level calculation, and defect reporting.

## 📋 Exam Rules (same as the real exam)

- Single best answer unless the question explicitly says "choose N".
- No notes, closed book.
- No negative marking — answer every question.
- Budget ~1.5 minutes per question; flag and revisit hard ones.

## 🧭 Suggested Use

1. Take one exam under timed conditions (60 minutes, no notes).
2. Mark it with the matching `_answers.md` file.
3. For every wrong answer, read the rationale and revisit the cited Learning Objective in the course.
4. Track your scores; aim for a consistent 85%+ before booking the real exam.
