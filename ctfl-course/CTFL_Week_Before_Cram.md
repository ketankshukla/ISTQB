# CTFL v4.0 — Week Before Exam Cram Plan

A day-by-day schedule for the final 7 days before your CTFL exam. Each day is designed to fit in 1.5–2 hours of focused study.

---

## Day 7 (One Week Out) — Foundations & Principles

**Goal:** Lock in the seven principles, test levels, test types, and the error/defect/failure chain.

**Study (45 min):**
- Read `01_fundamentals_of_testing/lessons.md`
- Read `01_fundamentals_of_testing/exam_traps.md`

**Drill (30 min):**
- Answer all 20 practice questions in `01_fundamentals_of_testing/practice_questions.md`
- Score yourself. Review every wrong answer and write down the rationale.

**Flashcard Drill (15 min):**
- Run through Flashcards Q1–Q20 in `CTFL_Flashcards.md`

**Evening (5 min):**
- Re-read the seven principles from the cheat sheet before bed.

## Day 1 (The Day Before) — Light Review & Confidence

**Goal:** Refresh memory without cramming. Build confidence. Sleep well.

**Morning (20 min):**
- Read `EXAM_STRATEGY.md`
- Re-read `CTFL_Exam_Cheat_Sheet.md` slowly

**Afternoon (20 min):**
- Re-read only the questions you got wrong across all 6 chapter practice sets and Mock Exam 1.
- Do NOT try to learn new material today.

**Evening (10 min):**
- Quick pass through Flashcards Q1–Q40
- Pack your ID, confirmation, and anything required for the test center
- Set two alarms. Go to bed on time.

---

## Exam Day Morning

**Before leaving:**
- Read `CTFL_Exam_Cheat_Sheet.md` one more time
- Eat a light breakfast
- Arrive 15 minutes early
- Bring: valid photo ID, exam confirmation, water (if allowed)

**During the exam:**
- Budget 90 seconds per question
- Read the question fully before looking at answers
- Eliminate two distractors first
- Flag hard questions, guess, and move on
- Use all 60 minutes — review flagged questions at the end

## Score Targets

| Practice Set | Target | Meaning |
|--------------|--------|---------|
| Chapter questions | 16+/20 (80%) | Strong grasp |
| Mock Exam 1 | 14+/20 (70%) | Pass-ready |
| Mock Exam 2 | 16+/20 (80%) | Comfortable pass |
| Mock Exam 3 | 17+/20 (85%) | Exam-day confidence |

If you are scoring below 14/20 on mocks by Day 2, spend extra time on the chapters where you are weakest before taking more mocks.

## What to Bring to the Test Center

- Valid government-issued photo ID
- Exam registration confirmation (email or printout)
- Layers (test centers can be cold)
- Clear water bottle (check center rules)
- Earplugs (if the center allows)
- Confidence — you prepared for this.
