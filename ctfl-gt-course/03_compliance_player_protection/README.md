# 🚀 Chapter 3 — Compliance, Regulations, and Player Protection

## 🎓 Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~25% (~10 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K2 (understand)
- **Focus:** Regulatory bodies, licensing requirements, responsible gaming, player protection measures, AML, KYC, underage gambling prevention

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Describe major gambling regulatory bodies and their jurisdictions
2. Describe the licensing process and compliance requirements
3. Explain responsible gaming and player protection measures
4. Describe anti-money laundering (AML) requirements in gambling
5. Explain Know Your Customer (KYC) and age verification requirements
6. Describe the role of testing in compliance verification

---

## ⭐ Key Concepts

- Regulatory bodies: UKGC, MGA, NGCB, state regulators
- Licensing requirements: RNG certification, RTP disclosure, game fairness audits
- Responsible gaming: self-exclusion, deposit limits, reality checks, time-outs
- Player protection: preventing problem gambling, protecting vulnerable players
- AML: source of funds checks, suspicious activity reporting, transaction monitoring
- KYC: identity verification, age verification, address verification
- Testing for compliance: regulatory test plans, jurisdictional differences
