# Chapter 3 — Test Leadership and Management

## Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~20% (~8 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K2, K3
- **Focus:** Leadership styles, servant leadership, coaching, team development, impediments

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Apply servant leadership principles to testing
2. Coach teams toward quality ownership
3. Build testing capability in teams
4. Manage impediments and blockers
5. Facilitate cross-team collaboration
6. Develop test professionals at scale

---

## Key Concepts

- Servant leadership
- Coaching vs mentoring vs managing
- Building quality culture
- Impediment management
- Team development (Tuckman model)
- Communities of practice
