## 📚 Study Techniques That Work

**Active Recall:** After reading a section, close the book and explain it out loud.

**Scenario Practice:** Create your own exam questions from the course material. Write a scenario, then write 4 answer choices.

**Agile Mapping:** Map every concept to your current or past agile team. How does your team handle testing? Where does it align or diverge from syllabus recommendations?

**Flashcard Sets:**
- Set 1: Agile values, principles, and their testing implications
- Set 2: Test levels in agile vs. traditional
- Set 3: ATDD/BDD/Specification by Example workflow
- Set 4: Test automation pyramid and CI/CD pipeline

---

## ⭐ Final Motivation

The CTFL-AT exam tests your understanding of how testing fits into agile teams and practices. The scenarios simulate real agile situations you will encounter. Every hour spent understanding WHY the correct answer is right is an investment in your agile testing career.

Good luck!
