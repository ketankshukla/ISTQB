## How to Use This Course

1. **Start with `STUDY_PLAN.md`** — Pick the 3-week or accelerated schedule
2. **Read chapters in order** — Each chapter builds on the previous
3. **Study `lessons.md` first** — Read the detailed explanations and worked examples
4. **Review `exam_traps.md`** — Learn the common mistakes before you make them
5. **Practice with `practice_questions.md`** — 20 questions per chapter with full rationales
6. **Do the case studies** — Integrated scenarios that span multiple chapters
7. **Take the mock exams** — Simulate exam conditions (40 questions, 60 minutes)
8. **Track progress in `PROGRESS_TRACKER.md`** — Know when you are exam-ready

## Target Audience

- Testers specializing in or transitioning to performance testing
- Test leads planning performance testing for their projects
- Developers needing to understand performance test principles
- Anyone seeking the ISTQB CT-PT certification

---

## Key Topics Covered

- **Introduction to Performance Testing** — Definitions, objectives, performance vs. load testing, non-functional characteristics
- **Types of Performance Testing** — Load, stress, endurance (soak), spike, volume, scalability, configuration, isolation testing
- **Performance Test Planning and Design** — Test strategy, workloads, scenarios, KPIs, test data, environment sizing
- **Performance Test Execution and Monitoring** — Ramp-up, steady state, ramp-down, resource monitoring, bottleneck detection
- **Performance Test Analysis and Reporting** — Interpreting results, root cause analysis, tuning recommendations, reporting
- **Performance Testing Tools and Automation** — Commercial and open-source tools, scripting, CI/CD integration, cloud-based testing

## Tip for Maximum Exam Success

> **Do not just read. Practice.** After each chapter, answer the practice questions without looking at the answers. Then read the rationales to understand WHY the correct answer is right and WHY the distractors are wrong. This active recall is the single most effective study technique for multiple-choice exams.
