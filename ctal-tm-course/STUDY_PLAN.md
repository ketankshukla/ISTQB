## 📚 Study Techniques

- **Active reading** — Take notes on test planning, estimation techniques, and risk management
- **Worked examples** — Redo estimation examples until you can calculate effort independently
- **Exam traps flashcards** — Turn each exam trap into a flashcard with the correct answer
- **Timed practice** — Practice questions under time pressure (2.5 minutes per question for CTAL-TM)
- **Error log** — Maintain a log of all wrong answers and review before each mock exam
