- **Estimated weight:** ~20% (~8 questions out of 40)

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Explain threat modeling and its purpose

3. Apply DREAD to rate risk
4. Build and analyze attack trees
5. Calculate and prioritize security risks

## ⭐ Key Concepts

- Threat modeling methodology
- STRIDE threat categories
- DREAD risk rating
- Attack trees
- Risk = likelihood × impact
- Risk treatment (mitigate, accept, transfer, avoid)
