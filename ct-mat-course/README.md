## ⭐ How to Use This Course

1. **Read `STUDY_PLAN.md`** for a realistic 4-week schedule
2. **Work through chapters in order** — read lessons, study exam traps, attempt practice questions
3. **Complete case studies** to integrate techniques end-to-end
4. **Take mock exams** under timed conditions (closed book)
5. **The day before your exam**, review `EXAM_STRATEGY.md` and the glossary
