# Chapter 1 — Introduction to DevOps and Quality

## Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~10% (~4 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K1, K2
- **Focus:** DevOps principles, CALMS, quality in DevOps, team culture

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Define DevOps and its relationship to quality
2. Describe the CALMS framework
3. Explain the Three Ways of DevOps (Flow, Feedback, Continual Learning)
4. Identify the role of testing in DevOps culture
5. Describe the benefits of integrating quality into DevOps

---

## Key Concepts

- DevOps definition and culture
- CALMS (Culture, Automation, Lean, Measurement, Sharing)
- The Three Ways (Flow, Feedback, Continual Learning)
- Quality at every stage
- DevOps vs traditional siloed approaches
