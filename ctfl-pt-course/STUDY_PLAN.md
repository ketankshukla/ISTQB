# CTFL-PT Study Plan

## Standard Schedule (2 Weeks)

| Week | Focus | Activities |
|------|-------|------------|
| 1 | Chapters 1-3 | Read lessons, study exam traps, complete practice questions |
| 2 | Chapters 4-6 | Read lessons, study exam traps, complete practice questions, mock exams |

## Daily Study Technique

1. **Read** the lessons for the target chapter
2. **Review** exam traps
3. **Answer** practice questions without notes
4. **Score** and review every incorrect answer
5. **Update** PROGRESS_TRACKER.md

## Scoring Milestones

| Milestone | Target Score |
|-----------|-------------|
| Chapter practice (first pass) | 70%+ |
| Chapter practice (second pass) | 85%+ |
| Mock Exam 1 | 70%+ |
| Mock Exam 2 | 80%+ |
| Mock Exam 3 | 85%+ |

## Exam Day Checklist

- [ ] All chapters completed
- [ ] All practice questions scored 85%+
- [ ] All three mock exams completed
- [ ] Glossary reviewed
- [ ] EXAM_STRATEGY.md reviewed
- [ ] PROGRESS_TRACKER.md shows green
