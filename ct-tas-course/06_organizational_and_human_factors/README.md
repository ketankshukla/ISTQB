# Chapter 6 — Organizational and Human Factors

## Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~15% (~6 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K2, K3
- **Focus:** Skills, training, organization, change management, metrics, communication

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Identify skills needed for automation
2. Plan training and mentoring programs
3. Organize automation teams effectively
4. Manage organizational change for automation
5. Define and track automation metrics
6. Communicate automation value to stakeholders

---

## Key Concepts

- Automation skills (coding, testing, architecture)
- Training and mentoring
- Team structures (centralized, embedded, CoE)
- Change management
- Metrics and reporting
- Stakeholder communication
