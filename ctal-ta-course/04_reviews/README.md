## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Describe the four review types (informal review, walkthrough, technical review, inspection)
2. Explain the review process (planning, preparation, review meeting, rework, follow-up)
3. Identify the roles in reviews and their responsibilities
4. Describe factors that contribute to successful reviews
5. Select the appropriate review type for a given situation

---

## Key Concepts to Master

- The four review types and their formality levels
- The five stages of the review process
- The five roles: manager, moderator, author, reviewer, scribe
- Entry and exit criteria for formal reviews
- Success factors for effective reviews
- Common review pitfalls and how to avoid them
