# ISTQB CTFL v4.0 — Complete Self-Study Course

## Overview

This course provides comprehensive preparation for the ISTQB Certified Tester Foundation Level (CTFL) v4.0 examination. It is designed for an experienced software engineer aiming to score 90%+ on the exam.

## Exam Facts

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Exam version | CTFL v4.0 |
| Questions | 40 (single best answer; a few "choose N") |
| Duration | 60 minutes |
| Pass mark | 65% (26/40) |
| Format | Closed book |
| Chapters | 6 |
| Learning Objectives | 64 |
| Business Outcomes | 14 |

## How to Use This Course

1. **Read the STUDY_PLAN.md** to understand the recommended schedule and sequence.
2. **Work through chapters 1-6 in order.** Each chapter folder contains:
   - `README.md` — chapter map, learning objectives, K-levels, exam weighting
   - `lessons.md` — full instructional content
   - `exam_traps.md` — common mistakes and ISTQB-favorite distinctions
   - `practice_questions.md` — 15+ original ISTQB-style questions with rationales
   - `worked_examples.md` (Chapters 4 and 5) — step-by-step technique application
3. **Complete the case studies** after finishing Chapters 4 and 5 to integrate techniques.
4. **Take mock exams** under timed conditions (60 minutes, no notes).
5. **Review EXAM_STRATEGY.md** the day before your exam.

## Target Audience

- Experienced software engineers who understand development but need structured QA/testing knowledge
- Professionals returning to the field who want efficient, exam-focused preparation
- Anyone targeting a high score (90%+) on the CTFL v4.0 exam

## Key Principles

- Every learning objective (LO) from the CTFL v4.0 syllabus is covered
- K-levels (K1/K2/K3) are shown for each objective so you know the expected depth
- Chapters 4 and 5 receive the most practice material (they comprise ~50% of the exam)
- All questions are original and written in authentic ISTQB style
- Content is based on CTFL v4.0 (not the older v3.1)
