# 🎨 Chapter 4 — Accessibility and Inclusive Design

## 🎓 Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~15% (~6 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K2 (understand), K3 (apply)
- **Focus:** WCAG, disability types, assistive technologies, inclusive design principles

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Explain the difference between accessibility and usability
2. Describe the WCAG POUR principles
3. Identify different types of disabilities
4. Describe common assistive technologies
5. Explain inclusive design principles

---

## ⭐ Key Concepts

- Accessibility vs. usability
- WCAG 2.1 POUR principles
- Visual, auditory, motor, cognitive, speech disabilities
- Screen readers, screen magnifiers, voice recognition, switch devices
- Inclusive design and universal design
- Legal requirements and standards
