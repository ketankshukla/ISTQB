# Chapter 5 — AI Quality Characteristics and Risks

## Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~15% (~6 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K2 (understand), K3 (apply)
- **Focus:** Fairness, robustness, explainability, ethics, AI risks

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Explain fairness in AI systems
2. Describe robustness and adversarial robustness
3. Explain explainability and interpretability
4. Describe ethical considerations in AI
5. Explain AI-specific risks and risk mitigation

---

## Key Concepts

- Fairness: demographic parity, equal opportunity, disparate impact
- Robustness: input perturbations, adversarial robustness
- Explainability: XAI, SHAP, LIME, feature importance
- AI ethics: transparency, accountability, human oversight
- AI risks: safety, privacy, security, societal impact
