# Chapter 2 — AI in the Software Development Lifecycle

## Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~15% (~6 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K2 (understand), K3 (apply)
- **Focus:** AI development lifecycle, testing challenges, MLOps, data engineering

---

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Explain how AI changes the traditional SDLC
2. Describe the AI development lifecycle
3. Explain the role of data in AI development
4. Describe MLOps and its relationship to DevOps
5. Explain testing challenges specific to AI systems

---

## Key Concepts

- AI SDLC: data collection, model training, evaluation, deployment, monitoring
- Data engineering and data quality
- MLOps vs. DevOps
- Non-determinism in AI systems
- Continuous training and model updates
