# 🎯 Chapter 1 — Introduction to AI and Machine Learning

## 🎓 Exam Weighting

- **Estimated weight:** ~15% (~6 questions out of 40)
- **K-levels:** K2 (understand)
- **Focus:** AI types, ML paradigms, neural networks, common AI applications

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Explain what AI is and its relationship to ML and Deep Learning
2. Describe the main types of AI: narrow, general, super
3. Explain supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning
4. Describe neural networks and their basic structure
5. Identify common AI application domains

---

## ⭐ Key Concepts

- AI hierarchy: AI > ML > Deep Learning > Neural Networks
- Narrow AI vs. General AI vs. Super AI
- Supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement learning
- Neural network layers: input, hidden, output
- Classification, regression, clustering
- NLP, computer vision, robotics, expert systems
