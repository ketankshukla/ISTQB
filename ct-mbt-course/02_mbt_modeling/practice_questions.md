---

## ✔️ Answers and Rationales

### 🔷 Q1
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** State transition models = systems with clear states and state-dependent behavior.

### 🔷 Q2
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Decision tables = business rules with multiple interdependent conditions.

### 🔷 Q3
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Filled circle = mandatory feature in feature models.

### 🔷 Q4
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** BPMN = business process modeling and stakeholder communication.

### 🔷 Q5
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Model quality attributes: correctness, representativeness, interpretability, maintainability.

### 🔷 Q6
**Answer: A**

**Rationale:** Unreachable state = logical error = affects correctness.

### 🔷 Q7
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Data movement and transformation = data flow model.

### 🔷 Q8
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Abstraction level depends on the test objective.

### 🔷 Q9
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Guard condition = Boolean condition for transition.

### 🔷 Q10
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Product variants with feature relationships = feature model.

### 🔷 Q11
**Answer: A**

**Rationale:** Representativeness = covers test-relevant aspects at appropriate detail.

### 🔷 Q12
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Early + stakeholder communication = high-abstraction model.

### 🔷 Q13
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Diamond = decision/merge in activity diagrams.

### 🔷 Q14
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Process models = UML activity diagrams or BPMN.

### 🔷 Q15
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Interpretability = understood by stakeholders.

### 🔷 Q16
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Condition combinations = decision table.

### 🔷 Q17
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Control flow = order of operations and decisions.

### 🔷 Q18
**Answer: C**

**Rationale:** Unexplained abbreviations = hard to understand = interpretability issue.

### 🔷 Q19
**Answer: C**

**Rationale:** Implementation details (database queries) should be excluded.

### 🔷 Q20
**Answer: B**

**Rationale:** Alternative = exactly one from the group must be selected.
